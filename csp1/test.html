TDKPH NEWS

Posted by Paolo Ollero on September 24, 2016

After 15 months of existence, TDKPh has officially launched their own website. It is still in the Beta Testing phase, as the admins of the group are still working on a few more features of the site. The admins ended up opening the site for Beta Testing in order to distribute our EXCLUSIVE BATMAN DAY KIT for Batman Day 2.0. However, rest assured that before Batman Day 2.0 at Comic Odyssey, The Fort, on October 01, 2016, the site will be ONLINE for all you, Bat-freaks.

Thank you for the continuing support to The Dark Knight Ph. All of this will not be possible without the love and support from the community.
